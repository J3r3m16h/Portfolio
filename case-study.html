Enterprise Vulnerability Scanning & Risk Mitigation

A comprehensive infrastructure audit uncovering critical misconfigurations and implementing zero-trust policies.

Kali Linux Nmap Active Directory

Role

Lead Security Analyst

Timeline

3 Weeks

The Outcome

Reduced attack surface by 45% and successfully patched 3 critical CVEs before deployment.

The Challenge

The client, a mid-sized financial services firm, was migrating their legacy on-premise infrastructure to a hybrid cloud environment. During this transition, visibility into endpoint security dropped, leading to potential unpatched vulnerabilities across their new Active Directory forest.

The Approach

I initiated a grey-box penetration test. First, I mapped the external attack surface using Nmap and Amass. Next, I ran authenticated vulnerability scans utilizing Nessus to identify misconfigurations in the internal routing tables.

  • Conducted OSINT to identify exposed credentials.
  • Executed privilege escalation tactics in a sandboxed environment to prove impact.
  • Audited the AWS IAM roles for excessive permissions.

Key Finding:

Discovered an unauthenticated SMB share containing backup configuration files, which allowed direct access to firewall rules.

Results & Remediation

Delivered a comprehensive 40-page executive and technical report. The engineering team immediately restricted SMB access and implemented a strict Zero-Trust Network Access (ZTNA) policy. Overall security posture score improved from C- to A within 30 days.
